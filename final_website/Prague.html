Explore Prague

  • Popular Sites

  • Old Prague

    • Old Prauge is known for it's old Eastern European architecture. Old Prague is filled with colorful buildings with artistic architecture. Also pay Prague Castle a visit.

  • Charles Bridge

  • Opened in 1402, this very old and famous bridge attracts visitors from all around the world. Walk through history across the Vitiva River, and view the historic architecture.

  • Entertainment

  • Street Performers

    • Prague is known to have many street performers displaying their talent Many street performaces are popular on the Charles Bridge because of the high amount of people who visit the site.

  • Pilsner Urquell Brewery Tour

    • Prague is known for Pilsner beer. Pay the Pilsner Urquell Brewery a visit and go on one of its tours.

  • Restaraunts

  • Bars

    • Prague is known to have several bars throuhout the city, and at a very low clost too! Enjoy a Pilsner with any meal.

  • Old Prague

    • Old Prague has several restaurants with fresh baked goods. You can smell it while walking in the streets.

  • Transportation

  • Metro

    • Prague's Metro only runs three different lines that can take you all around the city. It's a very easy system to figure out and understand.

  • Trams

    • Trams covers several ground in its downtown areas. Ran by electric cables and fixed on a track, you can find your way around above ground.
