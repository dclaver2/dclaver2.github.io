Explore London

  • Popular Sites

  • London Bridge

    • Reconstructed in 1973, this famous bridge crossings the Thimes River, and lights up London at night.

  • Buckingham Palace

  • Home of the British Royal family and Queen of England, this famous palace attracts visitors from all around the world, just to get a glance of its gates and outer walls.

  • Entertainment

  • Wembley Stadium

    • Home of Manchester United, this stadium can seat up to 90,0000 fans.

  • O2 Arena

    • The O2 Arena is known for hosting many concerts and venues of famous Musicians from around the world

  • Restaraunts

  • Kingly Court

    • This area in downtown London is known for its several bars and restaurants, as well as shops near by.

  • Oxford Street

    • Oxford Street is known to be the shopping district of London, but there are also many great bars and restaurants along the way.

  • Transportation

  • The Underground

    • The Underground is London's subway system, also known as the "Tube". This train system is a fast way to get around the city.

  • Bus

    • London is known for its double decker busses. Get a better view of the city streets from the second deck!





Bars and Restaurants

London has several bars and restaurants throughout its streets.

Abbey Road

Abbey Road was made famous by Abbey Road studios and the Beatles album cover. Now, it's a popular tourist attraction, and it hasn't changed in 50 years.

Big Ben and Palace of West Minister

Big Ben and The Palce of West Minster is one of London's amazing sites. Built in 1835, and adjacent to the Thames River, this site attracts tourists from all around the world.
